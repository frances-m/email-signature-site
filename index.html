Instructions

  1. Fill out the form on the left and click the Submit button.
  2. Ensure that the details in the signature above are correct, then click on the Copy button. For HubSpot and Outplay email signatures, use the Copy HTML button.
  3. You'll now be able to paste your new signature as needed. For further instruction, scroll down and click on the name of the app that you'd like to add your email signature to.
Microsoft Outlook (Web App)
  • Follow the instructions above and click on the Copy button.
  • In your browser, head to outlook.office.com.
  • Click the gear icon ⚙ in the top right corner of the browser window to open the Settings panel.
  • In the search bar at the top of the Settings panel, type "Email Signature" and click on the first result.
  • In the Compose and Reply tab, click on + New signature.
  • Enter a name for your new signature in the "Edit Signature Name" text box.
  • Paste your new signature into the large text input area and verify that your signature is correct.
  • At the bottom of the Compose and Reply tab, click the Save button.
  • Make sure that both of the drop-down menus in the Select default signatures section have your new signature selected.
Microsoft Outlook (Desktop App)
  • Follow the instructions above and click on the Copy button.
  • Open the Microsoft Outlook desktop app.
  • In the top left corner of your screen, click on Outlook, next to the Apple icon.
  • In the drop down menu, select Preferences....
  • In the Outlook Preferences window, click on Signatures.
  • In the Signatures window, click the + button.
  • In the new window that pops up, write a name for your signature in the Signature Name: text box.
  • Paste your new signature into the large text input area and verify that your signature is correct.
  • Click the save icon 💾 at the top of the window and close the window.
  • Make sure that both of the drop-down menus in the Choose default signatures section (New messages & Replies/forwards) have your new signature selected.
HubSpot
  • Follow the instructions above and click on the Copy HTML button.
  • In your browser, head to app.hubspot.com.
  • Click the gear icon ⚙ in the top right corner of the browser window to open the settings.
  • Under the General heading, click on the Email tab.
  • Under the Configure heading, click on Edit email signature.
  • In the new window that pops up, select HTML near the top right.
  • Paste your new signature into the large text input area and verify that your signature is correct in the Preview below.
  • At the bottom of the window, click the Save button.
Outplay
  • Follow the instructions above and click on the Copy HTML button.
  • In your browser, head to app.outplayhq.com.
  • In the top right corner, click on your profile image. Then select ⚙ Settings from the drop-down.
  • On the Settings page, click on Emailbox under the User on the left side of the window.
  • If you've connected your email account to Outplay, you will see your email address and its type near the bottom of the Emailbox page. On the right side of the page, across from your email address, click on the ⋮ icon. Then select Edit.
  • In the new window that pops up, select the </> (code view) symbol under the Signature heading.
  • Paste your new signature into the large text input area, then click on the </> (code view) symbol again and verify that your signature is correct.
  • At the bottom of the window, click the Save button.
